Who We Are

For 35 years, Cincinnati Early Learning Centers has provided excellent early childhood education programs to give children in Cincinnati, Ohio, the foundation for learning that they need. We're dedicated to working with children, teachers, and parents to ensure each student is excited about their education. We follow the NAEYC® guidelines for Developmentally Appropriate Practice, which are the basis for our program's day-to-day pedagogical practice. With this curriculum, we're able to give your child the building blocks necessary to start their educational experience off right.
